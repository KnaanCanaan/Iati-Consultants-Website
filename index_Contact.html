Iati Consultants

Iati Consultants provides policy research, analysis, and reports. We deliver quality reports, seminars and workshops addressing geopolitical, development and governance challenges in the Pacific. Our team employs catered methodologies to examine issues ranging from how to navigate the Pacific's strategic and security environment, governance institutions and systems, and sustainable development problems.

We actively assist our clients in understanding emerging trends, anticipated risks, and opportunities in this region and beyond. Our work informs strategic thinking by interpreting regional complexities and formulating clear and effective policy options. We empower informed decision-making for the fast-changing dynamics of Pacific geopolitics, governance and development.
